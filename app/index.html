Sustainability

The base for our battery is sulphur; sulphur is an industrial byproduct and abundant – no new mining is required. The production processes are inspired by nature and designed to be energy friendly.

We estimate 20x less energy needed compared to today's lithium batteries! We pledge to highest sustainability standards for other components (e.g. local lithium sourcing vs. shipping from Australia).

The battery is designed for recyclability and thus easy,
safe and cheap to recycle. Being sustainable enables us to produce cheaper and thus to sell at lower prices.
